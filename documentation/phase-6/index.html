I will start by explaining what an interpersonal conflict is.

In the case of Discord Moderation, an interpersonal conflict is a conflict between different persons.

This conflict generally appears as a dispute between people.

Many times this can result in something known as the "Halo/Horns" effect.

This effect is identifying people as simply good or bad without taking into account that people are much more complex than that.

This cognitive bias further worsens the relationship by increasing the amount and severity of conflicts that arise.

What can be an example of a conflict between two people?

To minimize and prevent conflicts in your server you must first figure out what is causing them to occur.

Maybe there is an interpersonal conflict between two mods because they have different moderation methods.

If that is the case, help them understand each other's logic and reasoning behind their different methodologies.

What might be the reason why one mod may warn for a meme while another one may do nothing?

Another big part of preventing conflict is learning everyone's opinion on it.

Introduce Democratic ideas like members and staff voting their opinions on aspects of the server.

This helps you learn their opinion on the matter.

You should also create personal relationships with as many people as you can in the server to understand their points of view before making decisions about anything.

There are many causes for interpersonal conflicts to arise.

As I mentioned earlier a difference in moderation methodology or even other ideologies or belief systems.

That includes religion and political beliefs too.

Competition is another common cause of interpersonal conflict.

Many people feel the need to compete especially in a staff team so that they can reach their goals fast and efficiently.

The biggest issue with this is competing with someone can make the other person look bad whether you are intentionally doing it or not.

So instead try to guide people to focus on themselves instead of how they measure up to others.

In events competing is perfectly acceptable as it is the purpose however.
