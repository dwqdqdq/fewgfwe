[PHASE 4] REPORTS AND SPAM PHASE

To begin, I will explain how this phase works! I will write 3 tickets as if they were issued by members of the server, reporting incidents both involving staff and members, and you will react to each ticket in the correct way! Then I will show you 3 examples of much more difficult situations, where the incident is oriented around something more difficult to judge! E.g. someone joking about something and someone taking it seriously where it's hard to determine if they were serious or not! In this phase, you are considered a staff member.
