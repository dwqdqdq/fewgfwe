Discord Moderator Training Facility Rules

What's DMTF? :crossed_swords:

Discord Moderator Training Facility, mostly known as DMTF, is a server dedicated to training users within Discord about moderation and in-depth management skills (as a manager on Discord).

How to stay safe on Discord? :shield:

  • :x: - Don't accept friend requests unless you know them.
  • :x: - Don't click on any links, even if they seem safe.
  • :x: - Nothing's free, don't get tricked.
  • :x: - Don't give out sensitive information. (e.g. last name, address, etc.)
  • :white_check_mark: - Have 2FA enabled.
